If you allow the anonymous statistics collection then the following information is collected and sent to us:

  • That you use the app, how many times it is launched and how long it is used for
  • What actions you peform (sign in/out, post a message, cluck)
  • If you are on wifi or your carrier network (but not which network)
  • What device you are using (iPhone, iPod Touch, etc.)

This information is only stored in an aggregate form, and will not be able to uniquely identify you or your twitter name, and does not include your location.

If you have any concerns, please do not hesitate to contact the developer.
