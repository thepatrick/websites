Introductions

I am a 26 year old guy who is currently working in Sydney, Australia.

I use Macs, I code in Ruby on Rails, Javascript and Objective-C. I avoid most other programming languages except C, and occasionally Java when Android is unavoidable. I have on occasion used Python. I promised myself never again. I renegged on that once. I hope not to again.
